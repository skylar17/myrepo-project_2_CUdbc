NYC Eviction Record - By Zip Code

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

See a combination box plot and scatter plot for each year between 2017-2019 to visualize evictions by zip code.

As shown, the number of evictions has been going down year after year. This could be due to a multitude of factors including tenant organizing, the work of eviction-prevention community organizations, and the roll-out of NYC Right to Counsel (RTC) in late 2017. RTC gives tenants with incomes below 200 percent of the federal poverty level who are facing an eviction in housing court access to an attorney.
